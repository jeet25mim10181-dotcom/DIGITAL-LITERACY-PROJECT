Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project was completed as part of the CSE0001 course. The aim of this project is to develop essential digital skills such as creating online content, building a professional digital presence, practicing coding, understanding online communication, and spreading awareness about cyber safety.

The project is divided into five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy.
My role is to help my peers navigate the digital world—from building a professional presence to staying safe from online threats. This repository is my official Digital Literacy Portfolio, combining forward-thinking tech skills with the traditional rules of professional communication.

Module Summaries
Task 1: Visual Awareness
I have used Canva to design to make an one-page infographic that will make digital literacy easy to understand. It covers safe internet practices and the basic tools every student needs to survive today.

Task 2: Building a Professional Presence
Your online profile is your modern resume and people will know abouty you from your profile. I have made my profile in GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to stay connected with the academic and professional community.

Task 3: Code & Cloud Collaboration
To keep my logic sharp, I completed a challenge on Hackerrank. I also built a Google Form quiz to help my batchmates and other to check their own digital literacy levels.Link of the form and response sheet is given below.

Task 4: Professional Communication
Learning to communicate professionally is a total game-changer and important in today's time. I have drafted formal emails—like asking for an extension or applying for an internship—that follow all the traditional rules of etiquette.I also made Social Media Do's and Don't checklist

Task 5: Cybersecurity Awareness
Online threats like UPI fraud are real in India. I have written a case study on how these scams work and how to manage and provided a prevention checklist with official reporting channels like Helpline 1930 to keep our batch safe.
